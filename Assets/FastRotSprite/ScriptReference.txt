ImageRotation - Performs pixel-by-pixel sprite rotation for Image (UI). Pixels outside the bounds will be ignored.
SpriteRendererRotation - Performs pixel-by-pixel sprite rotation for SpriteRenterer.
RotationBase - base class for ImageRotation and SpriteRendererRotation.
RotateOnStart - Rotates sprite on Start by specified degree.
RotateWithArrows - Rotates sprite with arrows (Input).
ScaleHelper - Performs pixel art scaling.
